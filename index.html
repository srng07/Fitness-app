frequently asked queries

How do I join?

It’s really simple. Click on ‘Buy Membership’ in the main menu and follow the instructions. You’ll have your membership in a few simple steps. Or come by the club during our staffed hours.

Can I try the app?

We propose trial sessions during our staffed hours. A trial session costs 25.- which is deducted from your membership if you decide to buy one afterwards.

Can I cancel my membership?

If you buy your membership on-line, and you’re not happy with the gym during your first visit, you’ll have 1 week after its activation to cancel the membership. Send an email to info@creativefitness.com during the first week, and we’ll give you your money back. No questions asked.

Can I pay later?

No, paying your membership on time is your responsibility. If you don’t pay we will block your access to the club, while your membership keeps running, and any future fees as well. As soon as we get your money due, we unblock your access again. If your debts are not paid within 1 month we will hand over your debt to a debt collector.
